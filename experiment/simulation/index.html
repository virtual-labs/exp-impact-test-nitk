IMPACT TEST ON AGGREGATE

Objective:

To evaluate the toughness of aggregates to break down under application of impact and to determine aggregate impact value of the given sample of aggregate.


Apparatus used:

Impact Testing Machine, Tamping Rod, Trowel, Cylindrical Mould, Levelling Rod, Weighing Balance etc.


 Description

Toughness: is the amount of energy per unit volume that a material can absorb before rupturing.

Impact value of aggregate: is a measure of resistance to sudden impact or shock, which may differ from its resistance to gradually applied compressive load.
